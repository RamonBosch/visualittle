How does it work?

Visualittle will ask you to choose between two options in 20 different situations. It’s an abstraction exercice in which you have to ask yourself what would your brand choose in a situation like that?

Brand personality?

Your brand’s personality are emotional traits and symbolic attributes associated with the brand itself, its behavior, its environment and its target audience. The good news is that a brand’s personality can be translated into visual choices to create a fitting visual identity!
