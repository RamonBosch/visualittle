How does
it work?

Visualittle will ask you to choose between two options in 20 different situations. It’s an abstraction exercise in which you have to consider your brand's personality to decide what your brand would choose in that situation.

What would
my brand do?

Your brand’s personality are emotional traits and symbolic attributes associated with the brand itself, its behavior, its environment and its target audience. Is your brand delicate and sensible or is it strong and mighty?

The good news is that a brand’s personality can be translated into visual choices to create a fitting visual identity! And your answers will help Visualittle do it!

Getting a
little visual!

Your answers will give Visualittle an average of its brand personality traits and turn them into matching design ideas that you can use to design your brand’s visual identity. As easy as that! Make sure to press the "NONE" button if no answer fits your brand's personality.
